REQ-ID: NFR-14
Description: The product will adhere to the General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2016/679). It allows the products data to be processed by foreign companies (e.g. cloud hosts from other countries) in a legal manner, the company will be accountable for any loss or theft of data that was not properly mitigated.
Type: 17 - Legal
Rationale: This new regulation will apply from 25 May 2018, wherein the Data Protection Act will be obsolete, we should focus on also adhering to the new Regulation. This shall protect customer and staff data that can be construed as PII (Personally Identifiable Information) or SPII (Sensitive PII).
Fit Criterion: Customers should not be able to see any PII/SPII without first authorizing with the application, Staff should not see any PII/SPII without first authorizing with the application.
